Hi, I am happy that you came across this small part of the internet!

This website is my small creative side project which I will use to share and collect interesting material. The different topics on this website might seem a bit random, but I have many diverse interests and would not like to limit myself to just covering one of them on this website.

The Story behind this website

For about a year I wanted to start a small side project, but limited time and the idea of not having something worth to share were keeping me from it. The main reason why I started this website anyhow was that with the help from the book “Show your work” by Austin Kleon I could convince myself that I don’t need something extraordinary to share but just that the process of sharing is worth it if it is fun for me.

Personal reasons for having a website

  1. Having a creative side project next to my studies and work.
  2. Storing knowledge and interesting links (e.g. for podcasts, recipes, machine learning,…).
  3. Practice writing.
  4. Learn HTML.
